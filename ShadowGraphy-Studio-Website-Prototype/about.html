About Us

15+ Years of Visual Storytelling

Our Story

Born from a passion
for real moments.

Shadowgraphy Studio was founded with one powerful belief: the best photographs are the ones where subjects forget the camera exists.

Led by Mr. Vicky Panchal — with over 15 years of hands-on experience in wedding and commercial photography — the studio has grown from a one-person operation into a full creative team trusted by hundreds of families across India.

We've been featured in WeddingBazaar, WedMeGood, Hindustan Times, and Wanderlog. Our work has taken us from the ghats of Maheshwar to the beaches of Goa to the deserts of Dubai — and every frame carries the same intention: to tell truth.

We also use cutting-edge AI face search technology to deliver personalised photo albums faster than anyone else — because we believe the joy of seeing your photos shouldn't wait.
